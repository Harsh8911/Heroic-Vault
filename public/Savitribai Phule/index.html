About Savitribai Phule

Savitribai Phule was an Indian social reformer, educationalist, and poet. She is regarded as the first female teacher of India. Along with her husband, Jyotirao Phule, she played an important role in improving women's rights in India. She is regarded as the mother of Indian feminism. She worked to abolish discrimination and unfair treatment of people based on caste and gender.
