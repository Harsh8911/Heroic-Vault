Savitribai Phule

A pioneer of women's education and social equality in India.

Birth January 3, 1831
Known For First female teacher in India and reformist educator.
Legacy Inspired inclusive education, anti-discrimination work, and social dignity.

About

Savitribai Phule transformed education by opening schools for girls and advocating equal learning opportunities for marginalized communities.

Social Work

She actively fought caste and gender discrimination and supported widows through practical social initiatives.

Enduring Influence

Her life remains a guiding force for feminist thought, public education, and human rights activism.
