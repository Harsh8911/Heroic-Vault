Mahatma Gandhi

Leader of satyagraha, non-violent resistance, and ethical politics.

Birth October 2, 1869
Known For Non-violent civil disobedience and freedom movement leadership.
Legacy Inspired democratic and civil rights struggles worldwide.

About

Mahatma Gandhi mobilized millions through peaceful protest and moral courage. His teachings made non-violence a practical instrument for social transformation.

Core Ideals

Ahimsa, satya, self-discipline, and public service shaped his strategy and his model of leadership.

Historical Impact

His methods influenced global movements for justice and civil rights, from South Africa to the United States.
