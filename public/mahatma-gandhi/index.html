About Mahatma Gandhi

Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi, popularly known as Mahatma Gandhi, was an Indian independence movement leader who led India to independence through non-violent civil disobedience. He inspired movements for civil rights and freedom across the world. Gandhi helped free India from British rule through peaceful resistance and is honored as the Father of the Nation.

Philosophy

Non-violence (Ahimsa), Truth (Satyagraha), Civil Disobedience

Movements

Salt March, Quit India Movement, Non-cooperation Movement

Legacy

Father of the Nation, Global influence on civil rights
