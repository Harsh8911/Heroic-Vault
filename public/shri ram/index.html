About Lord Ram

Lord Ram, the seventh avatar of Vishnu, embodies dharma and righteousness. He is the perfect example of an ideal son, husband, brother, and king.

His life story, chronicled in the epic Ramayana, teaches the values of truth, duty, and righteousness. He is often depicted holding a bow and arrow, symbolizing his role as a protector of dharma.

Cultural Impact

Symbol of ideal conduct and righteousness. The Ramayana has influenced art, literature, and culture across Asia for thousands of years.
