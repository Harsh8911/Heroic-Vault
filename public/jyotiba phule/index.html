About Jyotiba Phule

Jyotirao Govindrao Phule was an Indian social activist, thinker, anti-caste social reformer and writer from Maharashtra. He and his wife, Savitribai Phule, were pioneers of women's education in India. He led movements against the caste system and fought for the rights of peasants and other low-caste people.
