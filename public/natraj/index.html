Lord Natraj

The cosmic dancer of creation, preservation, and transformation.

Significance Symbol of rhythm in the universe and eternal cosmic motion.
Known As Nataraj, The Cosmic Dancer, a timeless form of Shiva.
Iconography Dancing pose within a ring of fire, with symbols of knowledge and liberation.

About

Natraj captures the cyclical nature of existence. The iconic dance reflects both destruction of illusion and renewal of life in one continuous movement.

Symbols

Damaru for creation, the ring of fire for cosmic cycles, the raised foot for liberation, and Apasmara for ignorance overcome.

Cultural Presence

Recognized globally as a classical symbol of India, Natraj bridges philosophy, dance, and spiritual science.
